TEAM HISTORY

Key Achievements

  • 2023: First Mongolian team to qualify for a CS:GO Major
  • 2022: Champions of Asian RMR events
  • 2021: Multiple top-4 finishes in international tournaments
  • 2020: Dominated the Asian circuit with undefeated streaks

From Humble Beginnings to World Stage

The Mongolz story begins in Ulaanbaatar, where five friends with a shared passion for Counter-Strike decided to form a team that could compete internationally. With limited resources but unlimited determination, they practiced in local internet cafes, often playing against teams from more established regions with better infrastructure.

Their breakthrough came in 2020 when they shocked the Asian CS:GO scene by defeating established Korean and Chinese teams. This victory earned them their first international tournament invite, marking the beginning of Mongolia's presence in global esports.

The 2023 Major Breakthrough

2023 became the defining year for The Mongolz when they qualified for the Paris Major - the first Major appearance for any Mongolian team in CS:GO history. While they didn't win the tournament, their aggressive playstyle and fearless approach against top-tier teams won them fans worldwide.

Their performance proved that talent can come from anywhere, inspiring a new generation of Mongolian gamers and putting their country on the esports map.

Team Philosophy & Playing Style

Known for their aggressive, fast-paced gameplay, The Mongolz play with a distinctive style that combines raw individual skill with unorthodox strategies. Their "never back down" attitude has become their trademark, often catching more experienced teams off guard with unexpected pushes and bold plays.

The team operates like a family, with players living and training together, building the chemistry that shows in their coordinated executions and clutch performances.
